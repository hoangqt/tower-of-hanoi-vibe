Interactive Towers of Hanoi puzzle. Use arrow keys to navigate between rods, Enter or Space to select and move disks. The goal is to move all disks from the left rod to the right rod, following the rules: only move one disk at a time, only move the top disk from each rod, and never place a larger disk on a smaller one.
When enabled, valid destination rods will be highlighted when you select a disk
